BERSERK

Berserk is a dark fantasy manga and anime that explores the brutal journey of Guts, a lone mercenary with unmatched strength and resilience. Set in a medieval-inspired world filled with war, demons, and betrayal, the story dives deep into themes of fate, ambition, and the struggle against overwhelming darkness. Its gritty art style and unflinching portrayal of violence are balanced by moments of profound emotion, making it one of the most influential and haunting works in the genre.
